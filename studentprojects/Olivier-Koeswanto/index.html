How to Shop Safer in Quarantine

Why is this a problem?

After Covid-19 hit, there's been a panic on how people can go out to get their daily needs while staying safe. The surfaces most products are from may contain Covid since it is known to live on platforms for days. Being around others may also pose a risk since Covid is mostly known to spread through droplets that are sent out from a sneeze or cough. Therefore going outside to shop may pose a big health risk to everyone.
