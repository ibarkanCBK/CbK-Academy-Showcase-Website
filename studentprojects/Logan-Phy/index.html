Noise Pollution and how it is negative

Noise Polution is a big problem, especially in big cities

Noise Pollution can be described as harmful amounts of noise. Usually Noise Pollution is present in large cities, like New York or Philladelphia, this may bring some issuses for people who may not be able handle the excessive ammount of noise; people like Veterans, Infants, and the Disabled. Noise Polution could affect even more groups too, but the three previous stated user groups are some of the most deeply afflicted.
