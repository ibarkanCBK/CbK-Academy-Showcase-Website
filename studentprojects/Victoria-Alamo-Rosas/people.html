People Impacted

who is impacted by this ?

- there is evidence that children in places like Bangladesh, Brazil, China, India, Indonesia, Philippines, Turkey, Vietnam are being forced to do child labor for the fast fashion industry.

- there are dangerous working conditions; factory fires, accidents and collapses are incredibly dangerous to garment workers.

- the clothing industry employs 40-75 million people. 80% is made by young women, who frequently face terrible conditions and abuse.
