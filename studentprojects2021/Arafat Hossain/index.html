Lack of Internet Service & It's Effects

Critical Access

Access to the internet is critical to be able to succeed in today's world. This is a problem because many people do not have access to the internet due to it being expensive or there being a lack of service in a specific part of the world. The internet is how people stay connected, gain information, and find new opportunities. Without it, people are unable to succeed.

Key Facts About the Lack of Internet Service

  1. There is a racial divide between those who have access to the internet as well as the speed of the internet available.
  2. Internet poverty has created a larger gap between healthcare professionals and those in need of aid and support. This has led to more individuals being unable to care for their basic health needs.
  3. Internet poverty prevents individuals and families from the ability to stay connected with family, work, as well as many other social services.
  4. Success in school and at work is directly correlated to access to the internet as well as having a good enough internet speed.
  5. COVID-19 has exacerbated and brought to light the glaring problem of internet poverty in Philadelphia. Due to quarantine efforts and school/work closures, the internet is how people stay connected. When there is a lack of access to the internet, it's accompanied by a lack of proper quality of life, especially during the pandemic.
