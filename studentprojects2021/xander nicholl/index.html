Gentrification in Philadelphia

Key Facts About Gentrification

  • Gentrification replaces the people who built the community
  • Gentrification changes the culture of the neighborhood
  • From 2000 to 2013, 57 different census tracts in Philly became gentrified.That is the fourth-highest figure in the country.

    • Gentrification is harmful to neighborhoods across the country particularly in Philadelphia where neighborhoods are losing its culture and people because developers and policy prioritize profit over people.
