The Solution to Poverty

Education

Poverty in Philadelphia is a widespread issue. People in poverty have limited access to basic needs. Families are trapped in the cycle of poverty: kids grow up without resources so they end up being in poverty as well. The way to break out of this cycle is through improvements in education.
