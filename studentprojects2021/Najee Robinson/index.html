Why Water Pollution is dangrous

Key Facts About Water pollution

  1. 30% Anthropogenic CO2 emissions are absorbed by the ocean
  2. Three times as much oil is carried out to sea via runoff from our roads
  3. We dump 17.6 billion pounds of plastic in the ocean yearly
  4. Our rivers, reservoirs, lakes, and seas are drowning in chemicals, waste, plastic, and other pollutants
  5. 80 percent of the worlds wastewater is dumped largely untreated back into the environment, polluting rivers, lakes, and oceans.
