Coronavirus restrictions in philadelphia

Key facts

  1. key fact #1 The city of philadelphia strongly recommends that all individuals wear masks at indoor public places, to avoid crowded indoor spaces,like parties or weddings. Unvaccinated and partially vaccinated people are encouraged to double-mask in indoor public spaces.
  2. key fact #2 Parks and outdoor activities are open fully vaccinated people are not required to wear a mask or social distance
  3. key fact #3 Philadelphia had loosened the restrictions for sporting events to let the crowds back,such as seeing the Sixers and Flyers play.
