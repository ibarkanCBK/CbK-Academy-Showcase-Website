Declining Air Quality in Philadelphia

Problem Statement

Bad air quality is an issue that affects everyone and can have devastating consequences.

Key Facts

  1. The ozone layer protects the Earth from the sun's UV rays however, it is slowly being damaged by man-made gases released into the air.
  2. Greenhouse gases mix with oxygen and water and create acid rain, which poisons the environment directly affecting humans.
  3. Greenhouse gases cause climate change which makes ecosystems change faster than animals can adapt causing extinction.
  4. Air quality has gone down so much that 9/10 people now breathe in polluted air.
  5. If greenhouse gas release continues, global warming could make the Earth uninhabitable and cause heavy damage to other species and ecosystems.
