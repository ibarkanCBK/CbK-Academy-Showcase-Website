People Affected

People Who Are Affected

  1. People all around the world, of the ages 7-18, of any gender, who live in any neighborhood, who have an education level between elementary school and high school, who look weak, who look smart or lonely, and who do not really stand out in the crowd.
  2. People all around the world, of the ages 7-18, of any gender, who live in any neighborhood, who have an education level between elementary school and high school, who look like they can not defend themselves, and/or are scared to speak up for themselves.
  3. People all around the world, of the ages 7-18, of any gender, who live in a bad neighborhood, who have an education level between elementary school and high school, who look small, who do not seem tough, and run home when they have to walk through their neighborhood.

Justified Claims

Based on information from my interview, bullying does lead teens to doing the unthinkable, evidence that supports my claim is that my interviewee told me that the people who bullied him did cause him to almost do the unthinkable but he said someone was able to talk him out of it before it happened. Some teens do not speak up for themselves when they get bullied, evidence that supports my claim is that there are school assemblies and commercials that tells you when you see someone getting bullied or you are, you should speak up, they are encouraging teens to do so because they know that not many teens speak up for themselves or for others.
