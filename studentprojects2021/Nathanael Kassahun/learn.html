Pros of Quitting

Quitting is the first step to restoring your body. Although it will be hard to start quitting, once you do you will notice many positive effects. You will find breathing easier, reduced chance of diseases such as lung cancer, it will be easier to focus and concentrate, reduced risk of heart attack, and after about 15 years your body will have fully outgrown the effects of smoking. The sooner you start, the sooner the effects will start to wear off and the sooner you can live life better.
