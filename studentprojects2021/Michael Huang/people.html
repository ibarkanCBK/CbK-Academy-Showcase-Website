Who Is Affected?

People that are in low-income communities and are attending a public school are the most affected by underfunded education. These are the people who have less resources and opportunities to do what they want. Parents and guardians of those attending a public school are also heavily affected as they are the ones who often purchase many of the supplies that students will need. Students, parents, and guardians of low-income communities are hit the hardest by underfunded education as lower-income communities will almost always have underfunded schools.

  • People in Philadelphia that have are attending a public school, highest level of education middle school or lower
  • People in Philadelphia that are a guardian or parent to someone attending a Philadelphia public school
  • People in Philadelphia that are in low-income communities
