Underfunded
Education

Problem Statement

Underfunded education translates to less opportunities and support for students.

5 Key Facts

Here are 5 key facts about underfunded education.

  • Lack of funding leads to less staff members and resources which limits the learning opportunities for students.
  • In many schools, teachers, parents, and children are required to buy necessary supplies for education.
  • There is a massive gap in the amount spent per student even within the same state.
  • Lower-income neighborhoods almost always have less funding for schools.
  • Extracurriculars like some sports and arts classes may not be adequately funded or may not even exist in some schools.
