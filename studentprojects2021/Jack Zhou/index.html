Smoking in Philadelphia

The Problem

Smoking is becoming an increasingly large problem in Philadelphia. Philadelphia is one of the cities with the most smokers in the U.S. Smoking causes many health issues, which can cost smokers a lot of money in medical bills.

Key Facts About Smoking

  • Cigarette smoking harms almost every organ in the body and causes many diseases.
  • Smoking is the leading cause of preventable deaths in Philadelphia and in the entire U.S.
  • Children/infants are more likely to suffer ear infections/asthma when exposed to tobacco smoke.
  • Tobacco cigarettes have nicotine in them which is an extremely addictive chemical.
  • On average, smokers die ten years earlier than non-smokers.
